Project Description

MP3 player can offer better sound trace quality and more professional functionalities on soundtrack playing area. Compared to other soundtrack play devices, users desire a more efficient and convenient way to purchase it. Smart Vending Machine, named as MP3 box, can provide such services. MP3 box, as similar as another vending machine, offers all different types of MP3 player for the user to purchase by operating touchscreen on the machine.

Requirements Summary

The primary purpose of the MP3 box is to provide an accessible way for users to purchase MP3. To archive this purpose, three major requirements are necessary, convenient location, safe environment, and comprehensive operation.

In part 1, we defined our task environment as public space with a large transient population such as malls, or supermarkets. The characteristics of these space are high accessibility for customers who have purchasing power. Therefore, the MP3 box can be served to more users in their daily life, and the first requirement, convenient locations are satisfied.

Second, since these locations are public places with high level of security, the various security measures, such as cameras, guards, and sensors in the facilities can be exploited for our MP3 box. So, our second demand, the safe environment is met.

Lastly, these locations have the bright lights, large space for the machine. These characteristics are advantageous to show many words on the screen. However, it can be vulnerable to leak personal information such as credit card information to others. Also, one of the characteristics of our possible users in previous assignment is professional, that users know or believe MP3 player is a better device to enjoy the music than other devices. To more specific, these users already understand what MP3 player is, and several possible features such as displaying lyric, equalizer, or FM transmitter. Although the users have domain knowledge about an MP3 player than others, every customer has a different degree of knowledge about each model, for example, full functionalities of every single model, and how good their sound quality is. Of course, some customers have sufficient experience and knowledge of the specific model.

Therefore, to meet our third requirement, comprehensive operation, and to complete part 2, we can summarize our environment and requirements as below,

  • Location Characteristics
    • Public and high transient population
    • Bright and roomy space to set machine
    • Noisy environment
  • User Characteristics - Common
    • Believe that MP3 player is superior music player than other devices
    • Having general knowledge on the MP3 player, with possible functionalities
    • Interested in sound quality of each device, and detail information
  • User Characteristics - Difference
    • Different degree of knowledge and experience of a specific model
    • Different purpose to operate the MP3 box, for example, to buy, rent, return or get information.
  • Task Requirements
    • Provide friendly interface for users so that every customer can easily interact with MP3 box
    • Figure out customer’s demand, for example, does he/she already bear a specific model in mind? Want to examine several models? Alternatively, want to get a recommendation?
    • Show customized information for each customer with sound testing for each device.
    • Keep security in open place during customer pay for the products.

Design Space

What requirements may be difficult to realize?

Providing customized information for each customer might be challenging to realize. For example, if a customer asks to the machine to recommend which product will be the best fit for him, machine have to find correct answers based on the insufficient information about the customer. It will require more complex technologies such as data mining, and machine learning. If we made a simple algorithm for recommendation system with several IF-Else statement, it is not likely the one which the customer wants.

What are some tradeoffs that you should or did explore?

We assumed that our possible customer has some degree of knowledge of MP3 player in general, then we design the friendly and simple interface based on this assumption. So we do not need to consider about explaining basic information of the device, such as what is MP3 or what is the advantage of using MP3 player. However, it is not clear that if we need to explain about detail specification, or functionalities, for example, what is the FLAC format, or what is FM transmitting function. Because some people do not know that information at all, even he has general knowledge of the MP3 player. So there is a trade-off between skipping information to reduce inconvenience for expert user and showing information for the relatively intermediate user to satisfy their desire to know.

Which tasks will be easiest to support? Which is hardest?

To figure out what is the purpose of customer who is using the MP3 box, will be the easiest to support. The machine just needs to receive customer request by touch screen button or voice, examine the database if it is required, then show the result on the screen. For example, if the customer requests the machine about the information of A product, the machine searched the database if A product exists, then show a description and asks if he wants to purchase this one. However, Implementing a sound testing function for every product. To make it correctly, we need to plug into each device with earphone and switch to another device to test it. Given those devices should be unboxed and the new one, it is hard to apply this method. Alternatively, we may make some sample devices to be opened to customers for testing. However, there will be space and price limitation to make sample devices for all products. And, we can record the every device playing sound and store inside the vending machine’s memory, then play with machine’s speaker. However, it is hard to guarantee that playing recorded sound is equal to sound quality of each device.

Design Summary

We considered three designs for this implementation, and two of them will be discarded, and the other one will be pursued for the next step. The discarded ideas focused on basic request and responded interfaces for purchasing MP3 players. If the product is a snack or soft drinks, this method can be accepted. However, given that MP3 player is relatively complicated and various product fields, the customer will not want to purchase it from traditional simple vending machines. In other hands, our selected design tries to combine these simple interface and recommendation system based on the customer’s preferences. This recommendation system, customers can feel like that they are talking with a real salesperson in the store because the major responsibility of salesperson is to suggest customized information to the customer. This advantage can be resulted in that satisfying our task requirements such as providing a friendly interface, finding customer’s demand and responding with proper information.

The Design A

The Overview of the Design A

This design focus on providing the functionality towards the soundtrack quality of MP3 player. Each kind of product will have one sample showcase for sound testing. The user can test the sound quality of the selected MP3 player by just clicking the button. If the quality is acceptable to the user, the user can go to next step to continue shopping. Otherwise, the user will continue to try other products.

User Scenario of the Design A

As one of jogging person, Mr. A enjoying listen to music with the small and simple device in his pocket when he is jogging in the morning. However, Mr. A does not want to walk around in the mall during the weekend for only one simple MP3 player. All he needs to do is to stop by MP3 box while he visits the supermarket for grocery as ease as following steps:
  1. Touch Buy button on the MP3 box screen as screen 1 shown above.
  2. View the product list as screen 2 shown above with personal preference by applying the filter.
  3. Check the detail information about the selected product, and test the sound quality of selected brand by clicking the sound test button as screen 3 shown above. If Mr. A satisfies everything about the selected product, he could click the buy button to buy it immediately.
  4. Input the personal information, for example, cellphone number, name whatsoever as screen 4 shown above.
  5. Select user preferred payment method, including debit card, credit card, or cash. By click pay button, Mr. A can get his MP3 from the MP3 box right away with an invoice.

Assessment of the Design A

Advantages

  • Simple to operate for all users
  • Convenient sound testing functionality for user
  • Technically implementable towards testing the sound quality of MP3 player
  • Flexible pay method for users

Disadvantages

  • Can’t provide feedback for producer
  • Testing the sample MP3 cannot guarantee the same quality of the actual product

The degree to which our requirements can be met by the design

The key requirements of the machine are to provide an accessible way for the user to buy an MP3 player without shopping in-store or online. By using this design, the main requirements can be satisfied.

Feedback from potential users when we observed and interviewed them

Most users think this design is acceptable to them for buying MP3. Also, all the basic functionalities, such as login, help, back the previous menu, are provided in this interface.

Comments

“It looks like very easy to operate, just like regular vending machine.”
“I prefer to use my credit card instead of cash for paying the MP3.”

Criticisms

“How can I be sure that the one I got has similar sound quality as the one I tested”

The Design B

The Overview of the Design

This design is focused on finding model quickly with two methods. First one is quick search which is typing model’s name or using filtered list. The second one is advanced search which provides graphical icons which make the user understand features more easily. Also, it has sound test functions with a headset, that users can listen to the recorded sound which is played by each device, so that user can get more detail information for purchasing.

Illustrations of the design

Screen 1 Initial page. If select Quick Search -> Screen 2 / Advanced Search -> Screen 5

Screen 2 To input model’s name -> Screen 3 / To show list by filters -> Screen 4

Screen 3 Based on user input, screen shows recommended model’s name. Select button will move to screen 9

Screen 4 Product lists with basic filter. Select button will move to screen 9, Sound test will move to screen 8.

Screen 5 Advanced Search shows graphical search option for exteriors and features.

Screen 6 For example, if user touches the SCREEN option, it will open more detail options to choose.

Screen 7 Based on the search condition, matched list is shown. Select button -> Screen 9, Sound test -> Screen 8

Screen 8 User can listen recorded sound of each device

Screen 9 User can select payment methods what he wants

User Scenario of the Design

Mark, 15 years old boy already has his metallic MP3 player. One day he heard from his friend, that there is a new MP3 player whose body material is glass. However, the friend could not remember the model. Mark got curious, rushed to the MP3 box in a nearby shopping mall, touched the advanced search to find the device which has glass material. Mark select body part on device image then select glass in the detail menu. Then he can see the list of MP3 players whose bodies are made of glass.

Assessment of the Design

Advantages

  • Easy to find menu or required part on the screen quickly
  • Make a customer, who does not want to see tedious descriptions or non-English speaker, feel comfortable.
  • Can find required information although the user does not know the correct name.

Disadvantages

  • Hard to explain detail specification which usually requires wordy sentences.
  • Possibility of misunderstanding of images.
  • The burden of making graphic resources whenever there are new features.

The degree to which our requirements can be met by the design

We assumed that our user would be the one either already bare a specific model in mind, or want to examine several models. This design can determine these user’s demand by asking quick search or advanced search. In advanced model, there are many graphical icons, so that they will increase the efficiency of finding. Therefore, the user can find a model quickly. The security problem does not matter in this case if we make covered number pad. However, there is a possibility that other person can recognize what the user is doing since graphical resources are readily shown in the distance. Although this design support sound testing functions, there is a limitation to provide customized information.

Feedback from potential users when we observed and interviewed our friends and colleagues in company

“I like this style; I just want to get necessity product as soon as possible without any cumbersome.”
“All right, but it seems like we should have some knowledge about MP3 player in advance.”
“I do not believe icon menu; sometimes it takes more time to understand what that means.”

The Design C

The Overview of the Design

This design focuses on making a friendly interface which feels like to talk with a salesperson. Based on the user’s selection, the machine will respond similarly to live communication in the store. This design assumes that consumer can be categorized into three types; just looking for a specific model, examining everything, asking recommendation based on his/her preferences.

Screen 7 Whole list of model with filter function. Select Model -> Screen 4

Screen 8 Select Priorities to get recommendation from the machine. Confirm -> Screen 9

User Scenario of the Design

Mary, the 57 years old lady, want to purchase a new mp3 player for her nephew, Tom. She knows what is the MP3 player, but not confident about what kinds of player her nephew likes. She heard that Tom prefers to listen to music with his big Bluetooth wireless headset. So, Mary thought that it would be safe if she purchases a device which supports Bluetooth connection. Mary went to the MP3 box, and fortunately, the device has an option that recommends the products based on her demands. She asks(in fact, touch the button) to the artificial salesperson that she only consider Bluetooth is supporting. MP3 box shows the list of MP3 players which satisfy that condition. Finally, Mary can purchase the proper present for her nephew.

Assessment of the Design

Advantages

  • Make user feels like being in the store with a salesperson.
  • As adding voice recognition functions, it will maximize user experience to be more comfortable.
  • Can recommend proper products based on user’s preferences.

Disadvantages

  • It can be a real salesperson who can understand and respond to any kinds of user requests unless perfect artificial intelligence appears.
  • For multiple user priorities, it is hard to make recommendation algorithm which can satisfy the customer. That can be solved by data mining technology.

The degree to which our requirements can be met by the design

As we imitate real salesperson, we can make a more friendly interface that user can easily interact with MP3 box. Also, the machine asks questions, and whole flows are similar to a general conversation in a real store. Also, it can recommend product’s list based on user’s preferences, so that it can provide a customized information for each customer. Like other design, it will have a sound testing function, and covered keypad to prevent leaking user’s private information. So this design can archive effectiveness, efficiency, and satisfaction of our task requirements.

Feedback from potential users when we observed and interviewed our friends and colleagues in company

“Salesperson in a vending machine is a little bit awkward, but I think it is much better than the boring interface of traditional touch, and voice-based machines.”
“I think the recommendation system will be great if it suggests what I like.”
“If there are more clever voice recognition system, such as “show me the pink, and strong enough to shock.”, it will be fantastic.”

An argumentative conclusion of which design is the best

Given our task requirements, we concluded that design C is the best option. Because this design is a user-friendly interface with artificial sales person like the real world, and with answer and question style conversation, it can figure out what is user’s needs, this design can provide proper information to the customer. Also, by suggesting the list of products that customer likely prefer, it makes possible to give customized information. Moreover, the idea of sound testing functionality give more useful information for decision making, and covered keypad can prevent from a security problem.

Requirements changes

We reconsidered the Part 1, to make a concrete analysis of the characteristics of location and user, then set up task requirements. Although we thought about making the sound testing function in the interface, it will be hard to implement in practice. So we need to delete that requirement. However, we found that suggesting proper recommendation based on the customer’s preferences will be the critical point that this machine can replace existing selling system by the salesperson. We tried to suggest the initial idea of that recommendation system during this design process. However, for more improvement, we need to consider about how to get essential information of the customer, and suggest a correct information that customer wants to.

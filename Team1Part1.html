Overview

MP3 box offers a easy way for people to purchase MP3 player on smart vending machine. People right now don’t need to visit MP3 player store for their favorite brand, or worry about the quality of the product by looking for a MP3 player online. MP3 box provides the full functionalities, such as renting, returning, purchasing. All the detail information and features about MP3 player or the process for any operations would be displayed on the touch screen in the MP3 box so that users could understand easily and conveniently on how to make a purchase successfully whatsoever. However, one important issue need to be fixed in such MP3 box, which is the soundtrack quality issue, that is how to help client test the soundtrack quality on each MP3 player.

Users’ characteristics

MP3 box is designed for people who prefer MP3 to enjoy quality music or soundtrack whenever they need earphone to perform their favorite music or soundtrack. So, most clients of MP3 box usually live in a city and can easily understand how to operate MP3 box with touch screen. There are three characteristics of users for using MP3 box, including:

  • Professional, which means clients of MP3 box still believe MP3 has better performance than other devices, including the size, the design, and the hardware.
  • Independent, which means clients of MP3 box usually have supportive financial income to purchase a device only for listening music or soundtrack.
  • Fast-pace, which means that clients require a convenient resource to provide desirable product instead of waiting for several days delivery.

Task analysis

Task Characteristics

MP3 box is a smart vending machine that has several features compared to other channels to get a MP3 player, including affordances, visibility, and conceptual model for user. User can easily access the system by operating touch screen to make a successful purchase as the following flow. If they don’t feel satisfying their purchased product, MP3 box offers very simple way to return their product with few steps. Also, support service is always for the clients who need technical problems, such as out of order machine.

Task environment

Considering the evaluability of the product in which MP3 box selling, the MP3 box will be mainly located at the mall or supermarket area so that not only people can easily access the MP3 box but also the product can remain secured all the time.

Structured task analysis of the problem

The following is a hierarchical task analysis of operating MP3 box successfully.

For customer choosing MP3 player instead of other devices because they believe that MP3 would provide the best soundtrack quality than others. Thus, it’s extremely important to check the soundtrack quality while they purchase or rent a MP3 player in the vending machine. However, the system doesn’t provide product trial during the whole purchasing process. It will cause unnecessary and inconvenient problems to users. But after he played the music in the MP3 player, he found that the quality of sound track performed by this payer doesn’t satisfy him, which he need to return it back to the box. However, for protecting our product from the abusive users, it would take one or two business days to handle such thing. This consequence will be seriously damage commercial business. So, it’s important to address such issue for the users and MP3 box business.

Strong Points and Deficiencies of the MP3 Box which is Assumed Existing

Strong Points

  • Time Saving
    As the value of time is more increasing than before for modern people, fast processing for any kinds of work is mandatory to stay competitive. Usually, purchasing a high-end electric device is time-consuming work, since we need to visit the store and consult with sales clerks to buy these things include the MP3 player. To be specific, we need to wait until one of the sales clerks come to us after finishing his previous sales, listen to many descriptions of several products, negotiation about the price, promotion, or service plan and so on. Although preliminary knowledge may vary on a person-to-person basis, all the customer should go through all the process until purchase decision. However, if we already decided for the specific target device, we do not need to endure this boring process. Thanks to the MP3 Box, the consumer can save their time without time-consuming. They just look for what they need, select, pay, then take it within a short time. Therefore, time-saving is one of the biggest reason for the necessary of the MP3 Box.
  • Accessibility
    To open a business store, the owner should consider its location. Since there are fixed costs such as rent, personnel expenses, or incidental expenses, the location of the store should be chosen to maximize their profit. Consequentially, the number of stores are limited, resulted in limited accessibility for the customers. However, when we consider using a vending machine, we just need small space to put it with less expensive managing costs. Also, owners can set several machines at several locations with the same budget to manage one store. That means the customer has more accessibility to get the service. With same reasons, if MP3 Box placed at many places, potential customers have more opportunity to meet this machine, and they can purchase MP3 player without efforts to search a store and move to there. Also, owners can make more profit from various sales locations. So, accessibility is the second strong point of MP3 Box.
  • Comfortability
    Some, maybe most of the customers feels uncomfortable when sales clerks try to start a conversation, even it is just for greetings. Usually, as the conversation is getting longer and longer, the burden of purchasing of the customer is also increased because of rewarding psychology. That can be resulted in buying unnecessary products. In this case, the customer gets a bad feeling from this experience, and he will have a negative stereotype of sales clerk although when sales person introduces bet fit product to the customer. This is not optimistic for stimulating the market and customer’s healthy shopping regarding economic. However, if there are many vending machines such as an MP3 box, customers do not need to worry about the burden of purchasing something. The machine just introduces, and show what customer wants, and only respond to customer’s request. There is no feeling of guilt when the client does not’ purchase anything after using the machine long time. In this reason, comfortability is the last benefit of using MP3 Box.

Deficiencies

  • Lack of Information
    Despite several advantages of MP3 Box, there are several shortcomings. First, the customer cannot get enough information about each product. Our assumed existing MP3 box has an idea of providing a simple description of MP3 player, but it is not sufficient to introduce full features of the product because most of the high-end electric device has a complex and long list of specifications and functionalities. Although we can show every detail of all the goods on the kiosk screen, it is not practical to read everything in front of the vending machine. Also, regarding time-saving, just displaying every information is harmful to get this advantage. MP3 player is relatively expensive product than any other products sold at vending machines. Not enough information cannot be attractive to the possible customer. Therefore, we need to find a way how to provide sufficient information with efficient way.
  • Wrong Purchase Decision
    Modern MP3 players have various features such as showing lyrics, playing video clips, synchronizing with other devices, supporting radio transmitter, wireless connecting, fetching digital multimedia from the store, and so on. Moreover, each feature can be broken down into more detail. For example, if we consider about the screen of MP3 player, there are sub-categories such as size, touch-sensing, color, ratio, and gesture recognition. Therefore, the customer should make sure what kinds of the specification they need for best purchase decision, and this is possible when we consult with a trained professional sales person who can match our demand and specific product. However, existing MP3 box cannot figure out our desire; then customer easily failed to purchase the good product. So, finding the best product for each customer during interaction is the one thing we need to overcome.
  • Limitation of Hearing Test
    We defined our customer as a professional person who believes an MP3 player is a better one to listen to music. So, quality of sound which supported by the device is a sensitive factor in determining to purchase. For example, we can easily see that many in-store music player markets are providing several headset or speaker to experience and test the sound quality of each device. That is the most significant difference between purchasing an MP3 player from vending machine and store. Therefore, we need to find innovational interface or alternative idea to let the customer test and listen each device’s sound.
  • Limited Payment Method
    We assumed that existing MP3 box could accept credit/debit card, and cash for payment methods. Given the high price of the MP3 player, those three methods are not enough to fulfill all the customer’s demand. The sales person in the expensive electric market suggests alternative payment methods to promote their sales and make feel better to purchase the product. For example, they can suggest an installment of payment plan through long-term, discount by registering promotional events, moreover, some store support cryptographic currency such as Bitcoin for more convenience payment. Therefore, we need to research if there are other alternative payment methods than a traditional vending machine.

The Larger Social and Technical System in which Our Design will Intersect

Social System

  • Individualistic Culture
    Although it is a not optimistic tendency, modern people do not want to be involved in person to person interaction in every situation in our life. In the past day, people had lived in small boundary for their life, so the social connection is critical since that is the only way they get the information. In contrast, we have too exposed to many human relationships thanks to an excellent transfer and communication innovation. Now People want to be individual unless it is not an essential part of our life. Purchasing product can be one of the situations which does not need to make a new human relationship. Using vending machine can give us an opportunity to be an alone from tired human connections.
  • Manpower Replacement
    As technology improved day by day, human labor can be replaced by a machine such as assembly robots in a car factory. Although it can make social problem such as decreasing hiring rate, we can concentrate on more valuable activity rather than simple and repeated labor. Also, it can give us more time to enjoy the recreational activity as a human. The vending machine can also contribute to this tendency to replace sales clerk with a machine which has more accurate and professional functionality.
  • Intellectual Satisfaction
    Since we can find many of information from the Internet, in some areas, we do not need a professional assistant in some field. Also, as we can see the success of IKEA which sells DIY furniture with low price, giving the opportunity to achieve difficult process by ourselves makes much enjoyment. Figuring out our best MP3 player from MP3 box based on our knowledge and acquired information will be a good chance to feel intellectual satisfaction. Of course, it is impossible and not so beneficial to understand every detail of all the products. So, if we can design a proper interface to provide best-fit information make the customer decide best products or understand specific knowledge of the device, the MP3 box can be successful.

Technical System

  • Integrated Information
    MP3 player is a small device but connected in various industry such as entertainment, display, sound player, product material, storage, battery, and so on. In respect to the customer, each person has a different background such as a preference for the music genre, color, size, or extra functions. So, the MP3 box should integrate all these information from the various source, and suggest possible options which can be acceptable to each customer. Therefore, the ability to combine data for products and customer to produce valuable information is the essential technology of MP3 box designing.
  • Big Data
    Relate to the previous integrated information; big data is the important technology of designing MP3 box. To suggest best options to customer, the MP3 box should rely on the previous purchasing history of other customers which has a similar preference with the current customer. Also, the machine also need plenty of information of MP3 players to make sufficient database. Therefore, big data technology is required to design good MP3 box.
  • Enhanced Interface
    The traditional interface of vending machines is relying on mechanical or touch screen buttons. That makes limited purchasing experience since the customer only has one-way interaction with vending machines. In case of our MP3 box, to figure out customer’s demand, and suggest best information are an essential part of our design, the machine should support more complex touch behavior such as gesture action on the screen for zooming up and out.

Initial List of Usability Criteria or Principles

List of Criteria or Principles

  • Learnability
    Many customers to our MP3 box will face challenging to understand how to use this machine since this kind of vending machine has not existed so far, and there will be several steps to purchase a product compare to the traditional simple vend-ing machine. Therefore, measuring how much new customer can start effective in-teraction with the machine and get a proper result is a critical factor to evaluate learnability of our MP3 box. For example,
    • Does customer understand all the interface component’s purpose and its re-sult?
    • Does customer understand how to input his request?
    • Does machine explain difficult terminology to make the customer under-stand?
  • Flexibility
    In case of in-store purchasing, we can always ask any question or do any behavior to sales clerk even he is talking about specific topics. Because we cannot make our decision in the strict process, the MP3 box should allow flexibility to the customer like a human sales person. For example,
    • Does customer ask query to the machine during any purchase stages?
    • Can customer skip any steps if he thinks that is not necessary for him?
    • Does customer have various options to input his intention?
  • Robustness
    The MP3 box should support features, make the customer feel the whole process and result are resulted by his intention and confirm the goal is achieved.
    • Can customer back to previous steps to modify his request?
    • Does machine show the proper response whenever there is customer’s input?
    • Does machine have a proper method to output the result corresponding to cus-tomer’s request?

How to measure

To evaluate our design, we will use query technique such as interview and ques-tionnaires. These two techniques are complementary each other. Although both are performed based on pre-fixed questions, interviewing can provide unpredicted in-formation, and deep insight of the problem, on the other hand, questionnaires can provide quick and quantitative measurement value to us. Therefore, once we com-plete the design of MP3 box in forms of storyboard or mock-up application, we can provide it to various people who have different age, gender, then conduct an interview and a survey.

Discussion of the Implications of What We Learned

After we discussed our new design of the MP3 vending machine, we figure out that the most important thing is to maximize customer’s satisfaction during purchasing or learning about the products. Moreover, it is only possible based on the detailed research on defining the characteristic of users, required task, social and technical environment, analyzing on the current assumed existing system.
Based on the research, we found that we need to consider how to convey essential information to get customer’s request and suggest best-fitted options to personal preference in short time without verbose. The information to understand each person’s liking is not only written text but also visual and auditory materials. Especially, we take note of the way how to make a heuristic interface that customer can get the direct experience of the devices. One of the ideas for this heuristic interface is providing a method for testing the quality of the sound of each MP3 players. We expect that this features can be extended more heuristic interface for any other device’s features.
